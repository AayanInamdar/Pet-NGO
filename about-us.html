About us

Where Every Pet Finds a Loving Home

About Us Pet In started as a small venture on October 5, 2021, when three friends came together with a shared vision of creating a better world for animals. What began as a pet shop blossomed into a full-fledged NGO in 2024, based in Pune. We are driven by compassion and rely entirely on donations to make a difference. Our work includes rescuing animals, administering rabies and other essential vaccinations, providing medicines for various diseases, and equipping pets with reflective collars to prevent accidents.

Currently, we extend our efforts to Pune, Mumbai, and Punjab, and have plans to expand our surveys and services to other cities such as Hyderabad, Karnataka, and Chhattisgarh.
