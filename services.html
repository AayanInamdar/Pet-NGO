Services

Services we offer

At Pet In, we offer a range of services to cater to your pet's every need. From top-quality pet care products to loving adoptions, we've got you covered. Our rescue initiatives also help street dogs and cats find forever homes. Our goal is to provide a happy, healthy life for your furry friends!

Pet product

At Pet In, we're passionate about providing top-notch pet care products that cater to your furry friend's every need. From nutritious food and tasty treats to comfortable bedding and interactive toys, our range has got you covered. We also stock a variety of grooming essentials, health supplements, and hygiene products to keep your pet clean, happy, and healthy. Our products are carefully selected from trusted brands to ensure quality and safety. Whether you're a new pet parent or a seasoned pro, we've got everything you need to give your pet the best life possible.

Addoption

At Pet In, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. We offer a wide range of pets for adoption, including purebred dogs like Labradors, German Shepherds, and Poodles, as well as cats like Siamese, Persian, and Maine Coon. We're also deeply committed to rescue pets, giving street dogs and cats a chance at a loving home.


Rescue pets may have had a rough start, but they're just as loving and loyal as any other pet. By adopting one, you're providing a home to a pet in need and creating space for us to rescue more animals. We ensure their health and happiness and support you as you welcome your new furry friend.

Rescue

At Pet In, we take pride in our rescue efforts, dedicated to saving street dogs and cats from harm's way. We provide essential care, including vaccinations, such as rabies shots, and medical treatment. To ensure their safety, we also provide light reflective collars to reduce the risk of accidents. Our goal is to give these amazing animals a second chance at life. We believe every life is precious and deserves compassion, love, and care. By rescuing and rehabilitating, we're making a difference, one life at a time.
