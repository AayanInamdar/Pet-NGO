Rescuing Animals: Give Pets a Second Chance Across India

At Pet in It, we are a dedicated NGO focused on rescuing dogs, cats, and all types of animals in need. Our mission is to provide the best shelter, treatment, and care for animals, including specialized medical attention, light-reflective collars, and rabies vaccinations. We adopt only those animals in urgent need, such as those unable to coexist with others, small puppies with a 20% chance of survival, or those in life-threatening situations. Operating in Pune, Mumbai, and Punjab, we rely on donations as we have no investors, and we are committed to expanding our rescue efforts in the future to other cities like Haryana, Hyderabad, and beyond. Please help us make India a better place for animals by contributing to our cause.
